Anti Money Laundering Policies

Money laundering is the act of converting money or other monetary instruments gained from illegal activity into money or investments that appear to be legitimate so that its illegal source cannot be traced. Dominica law makes it illegal for AURUM CAPITAL HOLDINGS INC., its employees, agents, or customers to knowingly engage, or attempt to engage in a monetary transaction in criminally derived property.

It is also illegal to transport, transmit or transfer, or attempt to transport, transmit or transfer a monetary instrument or funds if the purpose is to carry out an illegal activity, or to avoid reporting requirements.

Aurum Capital Holdings, Inc. views Money Laundering as a serious criminal offense, and as such, complies with regulatory requirements intended to forestall and prevent money laundering. These include:

  1. Verifying the identity of our Customers engaging in purchasing electronic currency, value added vouchers, or any other monetary activity involving fiat.
  2. Verifying the identity of our Customers applying to obtain our AurumXchange MasterCard as per MasterCard International Policies.
  3. Retaining transaction and identification records for a minimum period of five years.
  4. Training staff continuously in terms of anti-money laundering regulations.
  5. Appointing an officer responsible for monitoring and reporting any and all suspicious activities.
  6. Supervise and oversea that all transactions and information in the system is correct and complies with the Commonwealth of Dominica Anti Money Laundering applicable laws.

Please note that AURUM CAPITAL HOLDINGS INC. under aforementioned laws has the right to refuse a transaction at any time should suspicion arise that it may be connected to money laundering or any other criminal activity. In addition, AURUM CAPITAL HOLDINGS INC. will be obliged to report this suspicious activity in order to comply with said regulations, and internationally accepted laws and customs which also prohibits AURUM CAPITAL HOLDINGS INC. from disclosing this information.
